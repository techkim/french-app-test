Inheritance

Welcome to Inheritance - a free bilingual mobile app for learning French. Follow the story of Joe as he looks for clues to discover the mysterious treasure he has inherited from his great Aunt Jo.

Instructions - select highlighted words in the story to hear them spoken in French. Select the sound icon to listen to the whole page in French. Keep on the look out for clues to solve the mystery. Navigate using the buttons.

Credits - Inheritance was created by the Mme. Boulles 8th Grade French Class at St. Anne's-Belfield School in Charlottesville, VA.
