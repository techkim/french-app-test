Aromas Cafe Interview

What is your French connection?

Born in Morocco, Raised in France, Canadian citizen and proud American.

Is any of your family in the food services industry?

All my family is in the food services industry. My brother Paul used to have one of the biggest resturants in Monteal. My other Brother moved to South Africa and opened a restaurant called Carbon Café.

What is your favorite dish?

Cooking lamb. Braised slowly and could be served different ways over risotto or over couscous. That is my favorite dish

How do you recognize a good chef when you are looking for staff?

There’s a lot of different ways. The education, the school, the place where he did work, but I always give people the chance to learn. And and give him onions, see how he’s going to cut them.

And make an omelet which means, if you’ve seen the movie 100ft journey, remember when he make her the omelet and she hires him? It’s true in France if you don’t know how to make an omelet they won’t hire you
